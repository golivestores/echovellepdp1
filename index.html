WHY ECHOVELLE?

01

AAA+ Grade Akoya Pearls

We select only the top 1% of each harvest — pearls with mirror-bright luster, perfectly round shapes, and virtually flawless surfaces. Every pearl meets our uncompromising AAA+ standard.

02

18K Gold Craftsmanship

Every clasp, post, and setting is crafted from solid 18K gold — never plated, never hollow. Our jewelry is built to last a lifetime and become a treasured heirloom.

03

Hand-Knotted Silk Thread

Each pearl is individually hand-knotted on natural silk thread using the traditional Japanese method, preventing pearls from rubbing and ensuring security if the strand ever breaks.

04

Ethically Sourced & Ocean-Positive

Every pearl is traceable and responsibly harvested from certified farms across Japan and the Pacific — ensuring fair labor practices and minimal environmental impact. We give back, too: a portion of every purchase goes to clean ocean initiatives, and we partner exclusively with environmentally conscious pearl farmers committed to protecting marine ecosystems for future generations.

Pearl Origin

Japan

Japan is known as the birthplace of pearls and to this day still produces the finest quality Akoya pearls in the world. No other country can match the Japanese pearl farmers ability to produce the high luster and perfectly round shapes these classic pearls are famous for.
